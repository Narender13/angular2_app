How can I get a copy of my vehicle ID cards?

If you live in a state that requires ID cards, your ID cards are mailed to you with your policy forms when you become a Hanover customer and then upon each renewal. If you would like to order an additional copy of your ID card on-line, you can do so by following the steps below:

  • Select the policy that contains the vehicle you need the card for
  • Go to the "Vehicles & Drivers" tab, and
  • Select "Order your ID card"

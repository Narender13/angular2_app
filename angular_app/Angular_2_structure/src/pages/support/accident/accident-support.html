ACCIDENT SUPPORT

IN CASE OF AN ACCIDENT: Report all accidents to the Company as soon as possible.

Email Address: firstreport@hanover.com

Claim Reporting Number: 800-628-0250

AT The Scene of An Accident:

  1. Assist injured parties.
  2. Collect names, addresses and insurance information of all persons and vehicles involved in the accident.
  3. Do not admit fault. Make no payments or promise to anyone.
  4. Contact police.
  5. Call the Hanover/Citizens Insurance.

In case of Damage to home or personal property:
  1. Call police or fire department.
  2. Prevent further damage by making temporary repairs, if safe to do so.
  3. Secure all damaged property so a claim adjuster has an opportunity to inspect.
  4. Maintain all receipts for temporary repairs or extra living expenses.
  5. Call the Hanover/Citizens Insurance
